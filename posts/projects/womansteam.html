My contribution to women in STEAM

Empowering women in STEAM and creating space for new voices.

When I was young, I didn't have lots of female role models, and it took me a while to realize that technology was my field. That's why I decided to join Young IT Girls (YITG), a non profit organisation focused on inspiring young girls into STEM.

I was also a mentor in one of the editions of the Technovation challenge. I was mentoring a group of young girls who built an application to solve a problem. They decided to work on an app to help kids learn how to cook nutritive meals.

Seeing how some of the girls I spoke with already knew what they wanted to be when they grew up, or hearing them say after the talks that they liked math a little more now, was exciting and empowering for me as well!
